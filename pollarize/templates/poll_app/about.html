About us

Want to know what everyone thinks about the big issues? Have a burning question you need to ask the world? Need somewhere to copy and paste that 3 page essay you wrote about how Goku would absolutely destroy Superman? From the serious debate topics to the silliest arguments - for the college students, the marketing experts, and the hardcore DC Comics fans - there's a poll for everyone!

If you really can't find what you're looking for, you can always create your own poll!

The polls on our website are ranked by how "Pollarizing" they are (how close to a 50/50 answer rate they have) and the top polls are shown at the centre of our Homepage!

With a Pollarize account, you can:

  • Vote in one of our many polls!
  • Leave a comment after voting
  • Create your own polls and top the rankings!
